✨ One #Vivaldi gripe is that I can never remember how to rearrange the icons in my extension panel. They rarely change order, but every once in a while, I need to re-install an extension, and all ordering hell breaks loose.

Turns out, you have to hold down the ⌘ key, then click and drag on the extension icon before letting go of the ⌘ key and *then* your mouse button.
