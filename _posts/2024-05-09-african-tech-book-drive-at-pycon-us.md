My friend [Abigail](https://twitter.com/MesrenyameDogbe), is organizing a tech book drive for developers in Africa and is collecting them at [PyCon US](https://us.pycon.org/2024/) next week. These books will be given to high schoolers, university students, and early career folks.

If you want to donate any used or new tech books, please bring them to PyCon US, and let’s make plans to get them to Abigail.

For more details, please check out Abigail’s [tweet](https://twitter.com/MesrenyameDogbe/status/1788633911171383672), or feel free to reach out to me, and I can connect you via email.
