Hello! I'm Jeff Triplett. I work on Django and Python projects, contribute to open source, and help organize community events.

I'm a Partner at REVSYS. I'm on the Django Software Foundation Board. I co-founded DEFNA. I volunteer with DjangoCon US. I'm the past Treasurer and Vice Chair of the Python Software Foundation. I'm also an advisor to Black Python Devs.

I work on community and side projects like: Django News, DjangoTV, Django Packages, Awesome Django, and many more.

I'm always open to paid consulting through REVSYS or paid board advisory roles. If your organization needs help, please reach out to me.

If you aren't sure where to start, try my now page.
