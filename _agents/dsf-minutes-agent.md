An AI agent that helps users find answers about Django Software Foundation board meeting minutes through conversational queries. It supports natural language questions with optional year filtering and automatically syncs meeting minutes from the official django/dsf-minutes repository. Useful for researching governance decisions, financial matters, bylaws changes, and attendance records.
